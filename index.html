Methodology Notes

Gabor-Granger is ideal when product features are already fixed. Two behavioral biases offset each other:

WTP Understatement: Respondents intentionally understate their willingness to pay.

Purchase Overstatement: Respondents overstate purchase intent — mitigated via conservative conversion (Scale 5→60%, 4→40%, etc.).
